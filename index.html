Liquid Product and Gas Product

Liquid and gas products are packaged and contained in appropriate containers to ensure their safety during
transportation. Depending on the nature of the product, it may be packaged in drums, cans, bottles, cylinders, or
specialized tanks suitable for liquid or gas storage.
